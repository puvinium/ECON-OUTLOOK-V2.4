Data Sources & Methodology

Data Sources

BLS UNRATE — Unemployment Rate (monthly → quarterly average)
BLS CPIAUCSL — Consumer Price Index (monthly → quarterly average)
FRED A191RL1Q225SBEA — Real GDP Growth (%)
FRED FEDFUNDS — Federal Funds Rate
FRED GS10 / GS2 — Treasury yields, used to compute the term spread
FRED DCOILWTICO — WTI crude oil price (quarterly average)
FRED M2SL — M2 money supply (used to compute year-over-year growth)


Method

Each variable is predicted by an ordinary least squares regression with ridge regularisation (λ=0.15) applied to prevent overfitting against the limited training sample. Features are z-score standardised on the training set. A 2020 pandemic dummy variable controls for COVID-era extremes. The iterative forecast rolls each quarter's prediction forward as lag inputs to the next quarter. The Complex Multi-Factor mode additionally incorporates the yield curve, oil prices, and money supply growth — with exogenous variables held at their last observed level throughout 2026.
